================================================================================
  💡 HOW TO USE
================================================================================

  1. DEPLOY
     - Pick any option above
     - Upload the 2 files
     - Get your live URL

  2. CREATE PROFILE
     - Visit your URL
     - Choose username
     - Add display name & bio
     - (Optional) Add avatar image
     - Click "Create Profile"

  3. ADD CONTENT
     - Click "+ Add Content" in dashboard
     - Title (your content name)
     - Description (brief summary)
     - Content URL (link to YouTube, Medium, GitHub, etc.)
     - (Optional) Thumbnail image
     - (Optional) Tag/category
     - Save!

  4. FEATURE CONTENT
     - In dashboard, click ⭐ on any item
     - Featured item shows on your profile
     - Only one featured item allowed

  5. SHARE
     - Your public URL: site.com/#/yourUsername
     - Share anywhere!
     - Others can view your portfolio
     - They can discover your content
